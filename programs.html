Specialized Treatment Programs

Targeted rehabilitation programs designed for specific conditions and patient needs.

Pain Management Program

Most Popular

Chronic pain can take control of your life if not treated effectively. At First Physio, we provide a multi-modal approach to treating your pain without medication, with the goal of returning you to a normal life with improved function.

This Program Treats:

  • Chronic back pain & neck pain
  • Headaches & migraines
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Complex regional pain syndrome
  • Post-surgical pain
  • Neuropathic pain

Our Approach:

  • Comprehensive pain assessment
  • Manual therapy & massage
  • Electrical stimulation (TENS/IFT)
  • Frequency Specific Microcurrent
  • Exercise prescription
  • Lifestyle modifications

Arthritis Care Program

Joint Health

Arthritis doesn't mean you have to live with pain and limitations. Our specialized program helps maintain joint mobility, reduce pain, and improve your quality of life through targeted exercises and manual therapy.

Conditions We Treat:

  • Osteoarthritis
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Psoriatic arthritis
  • Ankylosing spondylitis
  • Gout
  • Degenerative joint disease

Treatment Includes:

  • Joint mobility exercises
  • Strengthening weak muscles
  • Pain relief modalities
  • Hot/cold therapy
  • Activity modification training
  • Assistive device training

Neck & Spinal Therapy Program

Spine Specialist

The spine is the foundation of all movement. Our specialized spinal therapy program addresses the root cause of back and neck pain using advanced techniques like McKenzie Method and Maitland mobilization.

Conditions We Treat:

  • Herniated/bulging discs
  • Sciatica
  • Cervical spondylosis
  • Lumbar stenosis
  • Whiplash injuries
  • Postural dysfunction

Treatment Includes:

  • McKenzie assessment & treatment
  • Maitland joint mobilization
  • Core stabilization exercises
  • Postural correction
  • Traction therapy
  • Ergonomic education

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Surgery Recovery

Surgery is just the first step. Our post-operative rehabilitation program ensures you recover fully, regain function, and return to your normal activities safely and as quickly as possible.

We Support Recovery From:

  • Total knee replacement
  • Total hip replacement
  • ACL/PCL reconstruction
  • Rotator cuff repair
  • Spinal surgery
  • Fracture fixation surgery

Our Approach:

  • Phase-based rehabilitation protocol
  • Pain & swelling management
  • Range of motion exercises
  • Progressive strengthening
  • Functional training
  • Return-to-activity planning

Senior Fitness & Mobility Program

Age 60+

Aging gracefully means staying active and independent. Our senior fitness program focuses on maintaining mobility, preventing falls, and helping elderly patients enjoy a high quality of life.

Program Goals:

  • Improve balance & stability
  • Prevent falls
  • Maintain independence
  • Manage age-related conditions
  • Increase strength & endurance
  • Enhance quality of life

Program Includes:

  • Balance training exercises
  • Low-impact aerobic activities
  • Gentle stretching
  • Strength maintenance
  • Walking programs
  • Home safety assessment

Parkinson's Disease Management

Specialized Neuro

Parkinson's disease affects movement, but physiotherapy can significantly improve function and quality of life. Our specialized program uses targeted exercises to maintain mobility and independence.

We Address:

  • Tremor management
  • Bradykinesia (slow movement)
  • Rigidity & stiffness
  • Postural instability
  • Freezing of gait
  • Balance difficulties

Treatment Approach:

  • Big amplitude movements (LSVT BIG)
  • Gait training with cues
  • Balance exercises
  • Stretching for rigidity
  • Dual-task training
  • Posture correction
