Sports Injuries Treatment

Get back to your game faster with specialized sports injury rehabilitation.

What are Sports Injuries?

Sports injuries occur during athletic activities and can range from acute trauma to chronic overuse conditions. Proper rehabilitation is essential for a full recovery.

Conditions We Treat

  • Ankle sprains
  • Muscle strains and pulls
  • Tennis elbow / Golfer's elbow
  • Shin splints
  • Achilles tendinitis
  • Hamstring injuries

Common Symptoms

  • Sudden pain during activity
  • Swelling and bruising
  • Limited range of motion
  • Weakness in affected area

Our Treatment Approach

RICE protocol and early intervention.

Sport-specific training programs.

Strength and conditioning programs.
