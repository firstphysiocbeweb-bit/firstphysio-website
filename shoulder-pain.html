Shoulder Pain Treatment

Expert physiotherapy for frozen shoulder, rotator cuff injuries, impingement, and shoulder instability.

What is Shoulder Pain?

The shoulder is the most mobile joint in the body, making it susceptible to various injuries and conditions. Shoulder pain can arise from problems with the joint itself, surrounding muscles, tendons, or ligaments.

Our specialized approach addresses both the symptoms and underlying causes of your shoulder pain to restore full function.

Conditions We Treat

  • Frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis)
  • Rotator cuff tear or tendinitis
  • Shoulder impingement syndrome
  • AC joint injury
  • Shoulder dislocation/instability
  • Bursitis
  • Labral tears
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation

Common Symptoms

  • Pain when lifting arm overhead
  • Difficulty sleeping on affected side
  • Weakness in the arm
  • Limited range of motion
  • Clicking or popping sounds
  • Stiffness and reduced mobility
