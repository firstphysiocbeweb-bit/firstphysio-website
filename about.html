Welcome to First Physio

Most people have their own Physician, dentist, advocate etc. Unfortunately most don't seem to have a physiotherapist to call their own. Perhaps it is time to consider doing so.

Physiotherapy can help you to prevent injury as well as rehabilitate you after being injured. At First Physio, we treat all orthopaedic injuries as well as neurological, cardio-pulmonary and sports injuries. We help you to understand how your condition is affecting your life. We teach you to restore function and achieve your goals. We get you back to work, to play and back to living.

At First Physio, we are committed to deliver individualized treatment in a safe, well supervised and comfortable environment, providing current techniques and our unique approach that will expedite recovery.

Why We're Different

Precise, Personalized Care That Works

Unlike traditional physiotherapy, which is often focused merely on relieving symptoms, our approach goes beyond temporary fixes and passive treatments to correct the cause of your problem.

We use advanced, evidence-based evaluation techniques to discover WHY you developed your bursitis, tendonitis or any other pain to correct that CAUSE and PREVENT IT from coming back - ever.

Our Physiotherapist is able to succeed where other treatments have failed because our advanced training allows us to focus on your body and where it is breaking down in your life. There are no standardized treatments for individualized pain. Because of this, our therapists work with you, one-on-one, to discover the best solution to your problems.

"We don't just treat your symptoms, we treat the cause."
