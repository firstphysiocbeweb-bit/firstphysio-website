Knee Pain Treatment

Specialized physiotherapy for knee arthritis, ligament injuries, meniscus problems, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

What is Knee Pain?

The knee is one of the largest and most complex joints in the body, bearing significant weight and stress during daily activities. Knee pain can result from injuries, mechanical problems, arthritis, or other conditions.

Our evidence-based approach to knee rehabilitation focuses on reducing pain, restoring function, and preventing future problems.

Conditions We Treat

  • Knee osteoarthritis
  • ACL / PCL injuries
  • Meniscus tears
  • Patellofemoral syndrome
  • Runner's knee
  • IT band syndrome
  • Post-knee replacement rehab
  • Ligament sprains

Common Symptoms

  • Pain with walking or climbing stairs
  • Swelling and stiffness
  • Instability or giving way
  • Difficulty bending or straightening
  • Clicking or grinding sounds
  • Lock in certain positions

Our Treatment Approach

Therapeutic Exercises

Strength and flexibility exercises for quadriceps and hamstrings.

Manual Therapy

Joint mobilization and soft tissue techniques.

Balance Training

Proprioceptive exercises for stability and injury prevention.

Bracing & Taping

Support and protection during recovery.
