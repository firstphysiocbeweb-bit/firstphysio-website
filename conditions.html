Conditions We Treat

Expert Treatment for Your Pain

From back pain to sports injuries, we provide evidence-based physiotherapy treatment for a wide range of conditions.

Common Conditions

Most Treated Conditions

Back Pain

Lower back pain, upper back pain, disc problems, sciatica, and postural back pain.

Neck Pain

Cervical pain, stiff neck, tech neck, whiplash injuries, and related headaches.

Shoulder Pain

Frozen shoulder, rotator cuff injuries, impingement, and shoulder instability.

Knee Pain

Knee arthritis, ligament injuries, meniscus tears, and patella problems.

Sports Injuries

Athletic injuries, sprains, strains, overuse injuries, and performance issues.

Hip Pain

Hip arthritis, bursitis, labral tears, and groin pain.

Our Process

How We Treat You

01

Assessment

Thorough evaluation of your condition, medical history, and lifestyle to understand the root cause of your pain.

02

Diagnosis

We identify the exact problem and explain it to you in simple terms, so you understand what's happening.

03

Treatment Plan

A personalized treatment plan designed specifically for your condition, goals, and lifestyle.

04
