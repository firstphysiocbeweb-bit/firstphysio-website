Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

First Physio Clinic ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website firstphysio.in or use our physiotherapy services at our clinic in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our contact form, book an appointment, or interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Appointment Details: Preferred dates, times, and reason for visit
  • Health Information: Details about your condition or symptoms (when provided through our contact form or during consultation)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referral source
  • Local Storage: Theme preferences (dark/light mode) stored locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and contact form submissions
  • To schedule and manage appointments
  • To provide physiotherapy services and care
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To send appointment reminders (if you have consented)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Web3Forms: Our contact form submissions are processed through Web3Forms. Their privacy policy applies to data submitted through our contact form. Visit web3forms.com/privacy for details.
  • Practo: Our appointment booking system is powered by Practo. When you book an appointment, Practo's terms and privacy policy apply. Visit practo.com/privacy for details.
  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to display text on our website. Google may collect usage data. Visit Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google Maps: Our contact page embeds Google Maps. Google's privacy policy applies when you interact with the map.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Contact form submissions are retained for the purpose of patient communication and follow-up.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time

7. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to save your theme preference (dark or light mode). We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. No personal information is stored in local storage.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
